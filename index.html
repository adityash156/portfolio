I'm currently an SDE Intern — AI/ML at Aivi Bandhure Solutions in Bengaluru, where I work on KNOI, an AI-powered educational content platform. My day-to-day is multilingual PDF processing: extracting Telugu, Kannada, and Gujarati textbook chapters with PaddleOCR and PyMuPDF, batching calls to the Groq API, and maintaining an evaluation tracker that categorises RAG failure modes across languages.

Before this, I spent a summer as a Machine Learning Intern at DRDO (PXE), Chandipur, building preprocessing pipelines over real meteorological field data and training XGBoost models for rainfall forecasting that fed directly into field decisions.

Outside of internships, I keep building: a knowledge graph over educational PDFs in Neo4j, a real-time anomaly detection service for a food-delivery platform, and a from-scratch rainfall model on DRDO's own dataset. I graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Parala Maharaja Engineering College, Berhampur, Odisha (CGPA 8.01/10).

What I'm looking for next: an SDE or AI/ML engineering role where I can keep working the full path from unstructured input — scanned documents, sensor logs, event streams — to structured, reliable output.
